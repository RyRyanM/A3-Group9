Group reflection

Working in a group is very common in high education. It's universally known that group assignments can be a headache to deal with. The skill is very useful not only for the university but also helpful in the workplace. Still there are some enormous challenges that we are facing when forming a group .In this last group assignment , everything seems to be very different . Although we still face numerous difficulties about how to organize the group work just like the last group work, the same problem does not happen with us. We already have some changes and improvements. The gigantic impact that our team positively changes is having a new teammate in the group.

To begin with, let 's talk about the weaknesses that our group still has. Our team is still facing lack of communication during the meeting. After dividing our job and asking some questions about the project our group usually remains silent for the whole section. Mostly, our group communicates through messenger by chatting. The other things that our group could be prevent to get better is get fully attendance in the meeting. Sometimes we just miss one to two people. But we can't all blame them. Since they might have some problem that they could not attend the meeting.To fix this problem after every meeting we post updates about the summary of the meeting so that anybody does not miss what was discussed in the meeting.

Despite the problem that I listed upward, all of the members did a very good job. As I say in assignment 2 our team still keeps up the fast working progress tempo. Maybe our group strategy is to use the discussion time to finish the individual work as quickly as we can. In the individual reflection part we all agree that Ayush plays a role as a leader in the group. Not only he motivates each member to do their task but also he is the one who sets our meeting times. All of the leader problems in assignment 2 just gone when we have him. Furthermore all of the members' contribution in this assignment is excellent.

We were very surprised how effective our group work is when we have a good leader. Not only is each member's contribution higher than last assignment but also our meeting time is also increased. The thing that we have learned in this assignment is how to work as a team and how to function properly. To be more specific, each member has different characteristics so we have to choose the best option to help the group work as smoothly as possible.
