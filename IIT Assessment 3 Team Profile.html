From initial observation, our group's ideal jobs are without surprise all related to Information Technology. The differences between each position vary as each persons’ job title are quite different from each other apart from the Database Administrator duplicate.

The online game designer position differs from the rest due to the nature of having “design” in its job title. Design generally covers a creative scope and usually involves going through an entire process from beginning to end of creating/ innovating a new game, brainstorming ideas and overlooking over the process to ensure that the initial intended blueprint of the game is completed.

Database Administrators is the role that stands further apart from the rest of the group in terms of similarity. The reason for that being, is because there is little-to-none knowledge of coding required in this role. Instead, DBAs control, manage and maintain databases of information for, when required, ready to use to customize database solutions to suit required needs. To do these things, a DBA is expected to have extensive experience with database technologies such as MySQL, MS SQL, PostgreSQL Oracle, MongoDB

Game Programmers focus on coding and troubleshooting, more of the behind the scenes of the functionality of the game and less on how the game would look as the visuals would be left up to the designer. They are expected to be one of the most creative minds around and should have Wide-ranging knowledge of gaming trends. In addition to that, they should have Strong analytical frame of mind. They should be able to to play with languages like C# and C++. Their main responsibilities are developing, maintaining and debugging game programs, ensuring that game applications meet functional and performance requirements. They should be able to work with teams such as designing teams to produce fruitful outcomes.

Website Developers put simply are creators of websites, covering a wide range of areas that a website would need to function properly and well. A web developer should be able to create websites that meet client’s requirements by using languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and other relevant web design coding languages. He has to work with graphic designers to produce some of the best outcomes.
